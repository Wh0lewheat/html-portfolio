Best movies according to Frank

My top 5 movies of ALL time

Blood in Blood Out

A movie about first generation Mexican American "Chicanos" growing up in the streets of East Los Angeles. The lives of three young Mexican gangbangers delve off into completely different directions

The Hangover

A group of 4 crazy dudes and their shenanigans from a bachelor party they were too intoxicated to remember.

Grind

A movie about some skateboard punks looking to get sponsored by any skateboard company and the rise to stardom.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Based on actual events, the story of the wildly successful Jordan Belfort and his rise and fall.

Titanic
